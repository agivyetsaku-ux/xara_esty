About Us

At Esty Agro, we empower farmers and agribusinesses with the tools, knowledge, and strategies to thrive. Through innovation and sustainability, we’re transforming agriculture for long- term success.

Get in Touch

Have questions or want to collaborate? Reach out to us for expert guidance, partnerships, or support. We’re here to help you cultivate success in agriculture.

Our Services

From Good Agronomic Practices (GAP) training to farm business consulting, we provide expert services that drive productivity, sustainability, and profitability for farmers and agribusinesses.

Meet our Non-Executive Directors

Mr. Chukwudi Joseph,

Mr. Chukwudi Joseph is an accomplished finance professional with over a decade of experience in finance and business management. Mr. Joseph has spent five years as a corporate and commercial banker and another five years serving as Chief Financial Officer across various financial institutions. His expertise spans SME financing, treasury management, corporate finance, consumer finance, commercial banking, insurance, and real estate.

Dr. Chris O. ECHE PhD,

FISDS, CPM

Dr. Moses Atewogboye, a seasoned agribusiness leader with more than 18 years of experience in multinational companies such as British American Tobacco, Lafarge Africa Plc, and ECOBasic Seed Company. He has built a reputation for excellence in sales, marketing, and agribusiness leadership. Currently, as Commercial Manager for West Africa at ECOBasic Seed Company.

Mr. Moses Atewogboye

Dr. Chris Eche, an astute agronomist with over 18 years of experience in field crop research, teaching, and production. Dr. Eche currently serves at the Joseph Sarwuan Tarka University as Head of the Department of Environmental Sustainability and Deputy Director of the World Bank- supported Institute of Procurement, Environmental and Social Standards (IPESS).

Dr. Jimmy Johnmark

Dr. Jimmy Johnmark, a distinguished professional whose career bridges the worlds of public health, veterinary science, and agricultural policy. Mr. Johnmark holds a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine and a Master’s in One Health from the University of Edinburgh, where he developed a holistic approach to human, animal, and environmental health.
