Full Job Description: Seed Production Supervisor

Location: Benue and Kaduna, Nigeria Reports to: Head of Operations Type: Full-time Salary: Competitive, based on experience Deadline: July 30th, 2025

About the Role

As our Seed Production Supervisor, you will oversee all aspects of field-level seed multiplication, ensuring quality, compliance, and yield performance across maize, rice, soybean, and other key crops. You’ll work closely with outgrowers, input suppliers, and internal agronomy staff to deliver certified seed that meets our eco- friendly and high-yielding standards.

Key Responsibilities

• Plan and manage seed production schedules and planting timelines • Supervise seed multiplication plots and monitor crop performance • Coordinate with farmers and outgrowers under production contracts • Ensure adherence to seed quality standards and regulatory requirements • Track input usage, crop health, and field activities • Collaborate with the soil and pest management team to maintain healthy crops • Support post-harvest seed processing, packaging, and storage • Maintain accurate records and generate regular field reports

Requirements

• B.Sc. or HND in Agronomy, Crop Science, or related field • 2–4 years’ experience in seed production or field agronomy • Knowledge of hybrid seed systems and varietal purity standards • Strong organizational and leadership skills • Ability to supervise farm laborers and outgrowers effectively • Proficiency in farm data collection and basic digital tools • Willingness to travel within Kaduna/Benue and surrounding production sites

What We Offer

• A leadership role in a growing agri-innovation company • Opportunity to impact food security and sustainability • Hands-on experience with modern seed systems • Career progression into operations and technical management roles
