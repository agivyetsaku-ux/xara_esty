About Us

Who We Are

Esty Agro is a forward-thinking agribusiness dedicated to reshaping the future of agriculture. We provide expert training, farm management solutions, and strategic consulting to help farmers and agribusinesses thrive in an evolving agricultural landscape.

Unlocking Potential in Agribusiness

At Esty Agro, we are committed to empowering farmers and agribusinesses with the right tools, knowledge, and strategies to thrive in a rapidly evolving agricultural landscape. By combining expertise with innovation, we help drive sustainable growth and long-term success.
Capacity Building – Training farmers and agripreneurs for long-term success Quality Seeds– We are NASC registered, to produce and distribute seeds in Nigeria Advisory – Helping agribusinesses make informed investment decisions. Technology Integration - Bridging the gap between traditional farming and modern advancements.
Isaac Joshua Ocholi (ACICRM) CEO, Founder Isaac Joshua Ocholi is an accomplished agronomy specialist and agricultural business strategist with over a decade of experience in the industry. As Founder and CEO of Esty Agro, he leverages his expertise in large-scale farming, farmer training, and agribusiness development to drive sustainable agricultural solutions. His leadership is built on a strong academic background and hands-on experience managing thousands of hectares and farmers with high success rates.
Prof. Ibrahim Muhammad Haruna Chairman of the Board Prof. Ibrahim Muhammad Haruna is a seasoned agricultural expert and Chairman of the Board at Esty Agro, bringing decades of experience in agronomic research, training, and sustainable farming practices. A distinguished professor, he has led groundbreaking research in soil fertility, crop management, and climate-smart agriculture, shaping policies and best practices in the sector. His extensive experience in academia and field-based research positions him as a key driver of innovation and capacity building in agriculture.
