Careers at Esty Agroscience

At Esty Agroscience, we believe agriculture is more than planting and harvesting — it’s about empowering people, building resilience, and growing sustainably. From field to farm to app, we’re building solutions that improve lives and transform Nigeria’s agricultural landscape. Whether you're an agronomist, soil scientist, marketer, or a tech whiz with a passion for impact — there’s a place for you here.

Current Openings

Junior Finance Officer - Kaduna

Support our finance operations as we scale across Northern Nigeria. This role is ideal for detail-oriented early-career professionals ready to grow in a dynamic agribusiness environment.
Don’t see a role that fits? We’re always open to passionate people with a heart for agriculture. Send a speculative application to info@estyagro.com

About Us

At Esty Agro, we empower farmers and agribusinesses with the tools, knowledge, and strategies to thrive. Through innovation and sustainability, we’re transforming agriculture for long- term success.

Get in Touch

Have questions or want to collaborate? Reach out to us for expert guidance, partnerships, or support. We’re here to help you cultivate success in agriculture.

Our Services

From Good Agronomic Practices (GAP) training to farm business consulting, we provide expert services that drive productivity, sustainability, and profitability for farmers and agribusinesses.

Seed Production Supervisor – Kaduna/Benue State

Help us scale high-quality seed production across Northern Nigeria. If you have hands-on field experience and a passion for crop science, this is your chance to lead impactful agricultural work.

Farm Manager – Lafia

Lead our growing commercial farm operations in Abuja. We’re looking for an experienced manager with hands-on skills in planning, team coordination, and delivering high-yield results across multiple crop cycles.

Seed Quality Assurance Supervisor – Kaduna/Benue State

Join our production team to ensure our hybrid seeds meet top agronomic and regulatory standards. From soil testing to post-harvest evaluation, your role is key to delivering trusted, high-performance products to farmers.

Sales and Marketing Lead - Kaduna

Drive Esty’s market growth and brand visibility across Northern Nigeria. This role is ideal for energetic agribusiness professionals passionate about sales, marketing, and making impact in the agricultural sector.
