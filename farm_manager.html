Full Job Description: Farm Manager

Location: Lafia, Nasarawa, Nigeria Reports to: Head of Operations Type: Full-time Salary: Competitive, based on experience Deadline: August 30th, 2025

About the Role

We are seeking an experienced and results-driven Farm Manager to oversee the operations of our rice farms in Lafia, Nasarawa. This role requires strong agronomic knowledge, operational leadership, and a commitment to sustainable practices. The ideal candidate will manage all field activities from land preparation to harvest while ensuring maximum yield and resource efficiency.

Key Responsibilities

• Oversee day-to-day operations across our rice farm sites. • Develop seasonal crop plans, monitor growth stages, and manage irrigation schedules. • Supervise field staff and casual laborers, ensuring high productivity and discipline. • Manage inputs, equipment, and farm logistics in collaboration with procurement and operations teams. • Implement best practices in pest, disease, and weed management in alignment with IPM. • Ensure timely planting, fertilization, and harvesting activities. • Maintain accurate farm records including yield, input use, and labor. • Coordinate with agronomists, consultants, and external partners. • Uphold environmental and safety standards across all operations.

Requirements

• B.Sc. or HND in Agronomy, Crop Science, or related field • Minimum 3–5 years’ experience managing a commercial farm • Strong leadership and people management skills • Practical experience with maize, rice, soybean, or similar crops • Ability to use farm planning tools, spreadsheets, and digital reporting apps • Willingness to work and reside close to farm locations within Nasarawa and Lafia

What We Offer

• Opportunity to lead and scale a flagship farm portfolio • Impact-focused work with visible results • Collaborative team environment • Room for growth into regional operations leadership
