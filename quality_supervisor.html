Full Job Description: Seed Quality Assurance Supervisor

Location: Benue and Kaduna, Nigeria Reports to: Head of Operations Type: Full-time Salary: Competitive, based on experience Deadline: July 30th, 2025

About the Role

We are seeking a meticulous and knowledgeable Seed Quality Assurance Supervisor to oversee quality control throughout our seed production lifecycle. From land preparation to packaging, you will ensure that every batch meets internal standards, field performance targets, and regulatory compliance. You’ll work closely with the seed production, lab, and field teams to maintain quality at every stage.

Key Responsibilities

• Conduct pre-production soil assessments to ensure suitability for seed multiplication • Develop and enforce SOPs for seed production, drying, storage, and packaging • Coordinate periodic field inspections and quality spot checks across farms • Monitor pest/disease risks and flag early-stage quality concerns • Organize germination and purity testing in collaboration with lab or third-party services • Ensure traceability from field lots to packaged seeds • Maintain quality documentation, batch reports, and compliance checklists • Train field staff and outgrowers on quality expectations and procedures • Collaborate with R&D and regulatory officers to update quality protocols

Requirements

• B.Sc. or HND in Agronomy, Seed Technology, Crop Production, or related field • Minimum 3 years' experience in seed production or QA role • Familiarity with hybrid seed systems, germination testing, and SOP compliance • Strong record-keeping and documentation skills • Ability to travel to farm sites and production facilities regularly • Attention to detail, analytical mindset, and proactive problem-solving approach

What We Offer

• A role central to our mission of delivering trusted seed inputs • Impact-driven work environment with field exposure • Training and support for QA tools and lab collaboration • Room for progression into regulatory or technical leadership roles
